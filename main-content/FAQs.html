FAQs

Home FAQs

Q. What if I do not need all of the services offered in one of your packages? Is it possible to ‘pick and mix’ services?

A. Before we carry out a piece of work with you, we carry out an initial review of your business, so that we can fully understand your financial goals and strategic needs. Once this is complete we can then prepare a bespoke package tailored to meet your precise needs.

Q. There are lots of accountants out there, how are you different?

A. Our goal is to work with you throughout the year, regardless of what SBM support package you choose, as all of our support packages get you one on one access with a qualified accountant.

By doing this, we are able to not only ease the stress that comes with keeping on top of bookkeeping, but we are able to free up your time so that you can focus on what you do best….Growing your business. Need help with that too? Then why not contact us here.

Q. I am just starting up my business and I’m really not sure what to do next, can you help?

A. In short, yes we can. Don’t let the fear of the unknown stop you or hold you back from starting your own business. At SBM, we can hold your hand at the outset and support you across all stages of business start up.

Still not sure? get in touch for a zero obligation chat on what to do next!

Q. We work in a very competitive sales environment and often struggle to keep our employees incentivised, can you help with that?

A. Absolutely. At SBM, we have experience of working with clients in similar situations and have helped them, not only by developing challenging reward and commission schemes but also by helping them to ensure that their sales strategy is aligned with their overall long term business goals.
